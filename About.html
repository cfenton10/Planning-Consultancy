Military Thinking Meets Modern Business

The Corporate Estimate is a planning consultancy built on the belief that businesses benefit most from structure, clarity, and operational precision. We bring the discipline and insight of military planning—specifically the Combat Estimate—to the corporate environment.

What Is the Combat Estimate?

The Combat Estimate is a battle-tested planning model used by military leaders to assess a complex situation, evaluate threats, allocate resources, and execute under pressure. We've adapted this model for business use, helping corporate leaders answer key planning questions with clarity:

  • What is the objective?
  • What challenges stand in our way?
  • What assets can we deploy?
  • How can we act faster and smarter than the competition?

Whether you're entering a new market, launching a product, or restructuring a division — our approach ensures you're operating with full situational awareness and purpose-driven action.
